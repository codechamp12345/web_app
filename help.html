College Complaint Registration

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How to register a complaint?

To register a complaint, log in to your account and navigate to the "Complaints" section. Click on the complaint type button and fill out the required details, then submit the form.

What information should I provide in my complaint?

Ensure you provide accurate details about the issue, including the date, time, location, and a clear description of the problem. Adding supporting documents or images can also be helpful.

How long does it take for a complaint to be resolved?

The time taken to resolve a complaint may vary depending on the nature and complexity of the issue. Our team strives to address complaints promptly, and you will be notified of any updates or actions taken regarding your complaint.

What should I do if my complaint is not resolved satisfactorily?

If you feel that your complaint has not been adequately addressed, you can escalate the issue by contacting the relevant department or authority within the college. Additionally, you can reach out to our support team for further assistance.
